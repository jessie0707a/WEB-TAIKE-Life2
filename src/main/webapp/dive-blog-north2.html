Northeast Corner 東北角 : Taiwan's Premier Diving Destination

Discover the captivating underwater realm of Taiwan's Northeast Corner, where the dynamic Pacific Ocean meets dramatic coastal cliffs, creating a diverse ecosystem of marine life and unique underwater topography. From the sheltered bays of Longdong to the thriving marine reserves of Chaojing, this region offers some of Taiwan's most accessible and rewarding dive experiences.

Best Time to Visit

The prime diving season extends from May to September, with water temperatures ranging from 24-28°C and visibility reaching 5-15 meters. Protected sites like Longdong Bay offer year-round diving, though winter months may experience stronger northeast monsoon winds.
