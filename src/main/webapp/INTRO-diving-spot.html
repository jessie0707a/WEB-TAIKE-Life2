Xiaoliuqiu: Taiwan's Hidden Diving Paradise

Discover the enchanting underwater world of Xiaoliuqiu, where crystal-clear waters meet vibrant coral reefs and gentle sea turtles glide through the blue.

Best Time to Visit

The optimal diving season runs from May to October, with water temperatures ranging between 22-29°C and visibility often reaching 15-20 meters.

Popular Dive Sites

  • Vase Rock - Famous for sea turtle encounters
  • Beauty Cave - Spectacular underwater formations
  • Secret Garden - Rich coral ecosystem
